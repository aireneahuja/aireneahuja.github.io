About Me

I am currently a grade 12 student and my main focuses in school include computer science, physics, and mathematics. As such, I intend on majoring in physics and computer science in university. I also have a passion for social justice! I want to change the world for the better and do my part in creating a more equitable world for everyone. I am always looking to incorporate all these things in my life in various ways.

Why Physics and Computer Science?

I've always loved solving problems and thinking with both logic and creativity to make that happen came easily to me. The questions found in both of these subjects allow me to apply these skills and challenge myself in different ways. I also love how physics can be used to explain and better understand so much of the world around us, and how computer science can be used in just about everything to make it more accesible.

Applying My Passions

I am involved in a lot of different things that allow me to apply my interests. I am a Math Challengers Coach, and I have been for the past two years. This involves teaching younger students and encourage them to challenge themselves through the subject. As a Physics Club executive, I help inspire a love of physics through fun projects and helping teach the rather interesting parts of it that aren't covered in regular courses

This is also my third year as a leader in the Gender-Sexuality Alliance. This club has provided an amazing oppurtunity for me to create a safe-space and be someone for younger students to look up to. The Dragons for Justice club gives me a space to work on creating change and equality, while making it accesible to everyone. Overall, I love being involved and expressing my interests in my community and beyond.

In My Free Time

One of my main hobbies is improving my programming skills through challenge problems and starting small projects. I like to challenge my mind and I have found that the best ideas come from free time and just spending some time on my laptop. I also love reading, and although I will read just about anything, I come back to a few genres time and time again. I love non-fiction astronomy books because anything to do with the universe, all of the weird and wonderful things that we don't completely understand yet, is incredibly interesting to me. Science fiction is also amazing because the books that authors, especially those with scientific backgrounds, write by incorporating science into their imaginations, are always a pleasure to read. While I don't get much free time for my hobbies at the moment, one of my goals is to make more time for the things I love!
